Shift & Status Logic

☀ Shift = Day

First punch hi decide karta hai — agar 7:00 AM se 7:29 PM ke beech hai, turant "Day" label mil jata hai.

🌙 Shift = Night

First punch hi decide karta hai — agar 7:30 PM se 6:59 AM ke beech hai, turant "Night" label mil jata hai.

✓ Status = Confirmed

Last punch bhi expected window mein aaya — Day: same din midnight se pehle. Night: agle din 9 AM se pehle.

⚠ Status = Single Punch

Sirf ek hi punch mila — first aur last same hai. Shift label first punch se mil gaya, lekin confirm nahi hua.
